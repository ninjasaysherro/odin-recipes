Cranberry Sauce

Cranberry sauce, sweet, tart, it brings a strong contrast to any Thanksgiving staple

Homemade cranberry sauce is a must-have for any Thanksgiving dinner. Made from fresh or frozen cranberries. You will want to write this recipe down somewhere. You wouldn't even spend a lot of time writing it down, it's that simple. Easy to make, easy to mise en place, and done in 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Sugar

  • 1 Cup Water

  • 4 Cups Fresh or frozen cranberries

  • Optional: Pecans, orange zest, raisins, blueberries, nutmeg, and allspice
